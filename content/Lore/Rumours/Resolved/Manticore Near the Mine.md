## Overview

According to [[Kiorna Kester]], a manticore has been harassing travelers along the [[Shepherd's Highway]] near [[Tin Tunnels Mine]]. Caravans have been delayed, shepherds refuse to move flocks through the area, and several attacks have been narrowly survived.

### Accuracies

- A manticore was genuinely attacking travelers along the Shepherd’s Highway.
    
- The attacks occurred near the Tin Tunnels Mine, using the surrounding hills and tunnels as cover.
    
- Multiple merchants and shepherds were injured or forced to abandon goods.
    
- The threat was real enough to disrupt travel and local trade.

### Inaccuracies

- None
