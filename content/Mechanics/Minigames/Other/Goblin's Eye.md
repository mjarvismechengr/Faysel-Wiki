## Overview

A game of darts.

## Rules
Players take turns rolling 1d20 and 1d6 both at once, three times. The d20 determines what number they hit. The d6 determines where on that number they hit. First player to hit 301 wins. If the score for a player exceeds 301, the score for the round of that player doesn't count. 

|d6 Number Rolled|Result|Point Multiplier|
|---|---|---|
|1|Outer Ring|Double|
|2|Black|Normal|
|3|Inner Ring|Triple|
|4|Black|Normal|
|5|Outer Bullseye|25 Pts|
|6|Inner Bullseye|50 Pts|

---

### Betting
**Minimum Buy in:** 2 gp

Winner takes the pot. 

### Example of Play 
2 Players join play. 

Players agree to bet of 10 gp.

<u>**Round 1:** </u>

**Player A:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 12
	- d6: 2
- Score from d20 lands it in the 12 point area, and a 2 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the black (no multiplier), so the score for the round is 12. 
- Total score is tallied: 12

**Player B:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 2
	- d6: 1
- Score from d20 lands it in the 2 point area, and a 1 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in outer ring (2x multiplier), so the score for the round is 4. 
- Total score is tallied: 4

<u>**Round 2:** </u>

**Player A:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 20
	- d6: 3
- Score from d20 lands it in the 20 point area, and a 3 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the inner ring (3x multiplier), so the score for the round is 60. 
- Total score is tallied: 12+60=72

**Player B:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 5
	- d6: 4
- Score from d20 lands it in the 5 point area, and a 4 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in black (no multiplier), so the score for the round is 5. 
- Total score is tallied: 4+5=9

<u>**Round 3:** </u>

**Player A:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 9
	- d6: 2
- Score from d20 lands it in the 9 point area, and a 2 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the black (no multiplier), so the score for the round is 60. 
- Total score is tallied: 72+9=81

**Player B:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 14
	- d6: 5
- Score from d20 lands it in the 14 point area, but a 5 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the outer bullseye (25 pts), so the score for the round is 25. 
- Total score is tallied: 9+25=34

<u>**Round 4:** </u>

**Player A:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 17
	- d6: 3
- Score from d20 lands it in the 17 point area, and a 3 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the inner ring (3x multiplier), so the score for the round is . 
- Total score is tallied: 81+51=132

**Player B:**
- Rolls
	- d20: 2
	- d6: 6
- Score from d20 lands it in the 2 point area, but a 6 on the d6 multiplier table means it landed in the inner bullseye (50 pts), so the score for the round is 50. 
- Total score is tallied: 34+50=84
  
  This continues until a player hits 301 exactly.
